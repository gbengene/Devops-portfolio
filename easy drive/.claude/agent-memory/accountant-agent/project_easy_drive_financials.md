Easy Drive operates as a two-sided marketplace (agent model, not principal). Platform fee revenue = 20% of host's listed rate, added on top. Revenue recognized at net (platform fee only), not gross rental amount.

**Why:** ASPE Section 3400 / IFRS 15 agent/principal test — Easy Drive does not control the vehicle or bear inventory risk.
**How to apply:** Income statement shows platform fee revenue only (~$70 on a $350 rental). Never gross up to the full rental amount.

## HST
- 13% HST applies to platform fees only (Easy Drive is the registered entity).
- Hosts are likely below the $30,000 small supplier threshold for a single vehicle; no HST on host rental rate unless host is independently registered.
- HST collected on platform fee must be remitted quarterly via CRA My Business Account.

## Stripe Connect
- Stripe fees (~2.9% + $0.30) apply to the gross charge (host rate + platform fee + deposit).
- Stripe fees borne by the platform, not passed to hosts.
- Business plan's $80–$200/month Stripe fee estimate is understated; corrected estimate is $250–$400/month at 10 cars.

## Security Deposits
- $500 deposit = liability (Security Deposits Held), never income.
- Released to renter on clean return; applied to host on damage.
- Forfeited deposit paid through to host — not retained as Easy Drive income unless a mediation fee is introduced.

## T4A Obligations
- Hosts earning $500+ per year from the platform require T4A slips (due February 28 of following year).
- Collect SIN (or BN if incorporated) from every host at onboarding — mandatory field before first payout.
- CPA memo needed on whether host payouts are "fees for services" (Box 48) or "rent" — affects T4A box used.

## Year 1 P&L Summary (Revised Model)
- Break-even at ~4–5 active cars (corrected from business plan's 2 cars).
- Month 1 expected loss ~$444; Month 2 breakeven at 6 cars.
- Year 1 projected net profit: ~$31,455 pre-tax; ~$27,615 after ~12.2% corporate tax (SBD rate).
- Fixed costs corrected to ~$960/month (business plan understated at $450).

## Bookkeeping
- Wave acceptable at launch (Months 1–3); migrate to QuickBooks Online by Month 4–6.
- T4A prep requires separate process (PaymentEvolution or CPA).
